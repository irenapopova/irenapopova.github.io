This page outlines the principles, themes, and working directions that inform my research and academic writing.

## Research Focus

My work connects applied linguistics, multilingual education, digital learning, and emerging technologies. I am especially interested in the relationship between language, cognition, structured learning, and computational thinking.

## Core Themes

* Artificial intelligence in education
* Natural language processing and language learning
* Applied linguistics and grammar awareness
* Multilingualism and adult education
* Computational thinking and programming education
* Blockchain and emerging technologies

## Working Approach

My research is guided by clarity, interdisciplinary thinking, and practical relevance. I aim to connect theoretical insight with educational application, especially in contexts where language, technology, and learning processes intersect.

## Ongoing Development

This page serves as a living space for research direction, academic framing, and future project development. It may continue to evolve alongside publications, working papers, and project-based writing.
