## Academic Profile

Interdisciplinary researcher with a background in applied linguistics, multilingual education, software engineering, and digital learning. My work explores the relationships among language, cognition, knowledge representation, computational thinking, and human-centred artificial intelligence.

I am particularly interested in multilingual natural language processing, data integration, knowledge extraction, explainable AI, digital education, and the design of intelligent systems that support interpretation, learning, and human decision-making. My professional experience combines academic writing, language education, informatics teaching, Python and JavaScript development, and the implementation of digital learning and knowledge platforms.

Research Interests
Human-centred and explainable artificial intelligence
